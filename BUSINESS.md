# BUSINESS.md

## Who We Serve

Catalyst Web Systems is built for US-based small service businesses where trust, speed, and reliable follow-up directly influence revenue. Typical clients include HVAC, plumbing, landscaping, cleaning, pest control, roofing, remodeling, and similar trade businesses that need both a stronger digital presence and systems that keep every lead moving.

## Value Proposition

- **Decision clarity:** We align messaging, offers, and trust cues to how local buyers search, compare, and decide, so the website immediately communicates credibility.
- **Full response confidence:** Every inquiry is captured, routed, and followed up automatically through standard tooling (Cal.com, HubSpot, Zapier/Make, Tally) so you never miss the moment when a lead is hot.
- **Ownership-first delivery:** Domains, data, automations, and tooling are delivered in client accounts—no black-box lock-in, no hidden handoffs, no decision-makers you cannot reach.
- **Speed without sacrifice:** Lean scopes, clear approvals, and focused build sprints mean a polished result in 5–10 business days for most packages.
- **Long-term clarity:** After launch we keep the relationship direct, transparent, and aligned to real usage so recommendations stay honest, tooling stays maintainable, and change stays practical.

## Core Services

### Website Builds

Websites that look premium, load fast, and convert visitors into calls. Builds include:
- Desktop, tablet, and mobile responsiveness with SEO-ready architecture.
- Clear call-to-action pathways, trust signals, and concise offer framing tailored to the client’s service areas.
- Hosting, SSL, and maintenance operations managed on behalf of the client when requested.
- Standard measurement and reporting so you know how many leads the site is producing.

Typical tiers:
- *Spark* — polished 3-page launch with hosting and maintenance. Price is $1,099.
- *Ignite* — expanded site (up to seven dynamic pages) with booking/reservation flows, analytics, and conversion optimizations. Price is $2,799.
- *Catalyst* — fully custom platform (ecommerce, portals, API integrations) intended as a long-term operational hub. Price is custom.

### Lead Automations

Every lead is followed up within minutes, not hours, through automated SMS and email sequences. Capabilities include:
- Automated capture of form submissions, calls, and direct inquiries.
- CRM routing with HubSpot (or similar) so every lead lands in the inbox you prefer.
- Review requests, nurture sequences, and reminders that keep prospects moving down the funnel.
- Integration with Zapier/Make for handoffs, data syncing, and notifications across tools.
- Operational dashboards that show delivery status without additional legwork.

### Automation Systems

Custom automations and operational plumbing built around the tools already in use. This service covers:
- Workflow audits that identify the exact bottlenecks before any automation is built.
- System design to align the website, intake paths, notifications, and reporting.
- Tool integrations across CRMs, calendars, job boards, email, and messaging platforms.
- Ongoing refinement to reflect actual business growth, not hypothetical features.
- Scoped partnerships that can grow into managed support when automation needs scale.

## Engagement Model

1. **We listen first.** A short, pressure-free call reveals the current offer, marketing gaps, and lead-handling pain points.
2. **We define the system.** Scope, page structure, forms, routing, and ownership are agreed before any code is written.
3. **We build fast.** Production-ready websites or automations launch in 5–10 business days with client sign-off on every milestone.
4. **We keep it open.** Domains, data, and tools remain under your control; we stay available for updates or recommendations without locking you in.
5. **We stay honest.** If the next best step sits outside our focus, we explain why instead of overselling a broader package.

## Outcomes

- Clarity for prospects when they visit your website.
- Consistent, measurable response times for every lead.
- Systems that are visible, maintainable, and ready for the next phase of growth.
